How to Become a Member

Interested in building confidence, sharpening your speaking skills, and engaging in meaningful discussions? Fill out the form to become a member of our debate club! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced debater, everyone is welcome.
Once you sign up, we’ll reach out with more information about upcoming meetings, training sessions, and how to get involved in debates and competitions. We can’t wait to hear your voice!

As a member, you'll have access to exclusive resources, regular practice sessions, and opportunities to represent the club in school and inter-school competitions. We also offer mentorship and feedback to help you improve with every debate. Signing up is just the first step—your journey as a confident, persuasive speaker starts here!
